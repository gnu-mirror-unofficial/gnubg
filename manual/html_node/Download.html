11.1.2 Download

You may download the two sided database with 6 checkers on 6 points from ftp://alpha.gnu.org/gnu/gnubg/gnubg_ts0.bd.gz and the one sided database with 15 checkers 6 points from ftp://alpha.gnu.org/gnu/gnubg/gnubg_os0.bd.gz.

Later, other databases may be available for download or it may be possible to purchase these on CDROM or DVD for a nominal fee.
