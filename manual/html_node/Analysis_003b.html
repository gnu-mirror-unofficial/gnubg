10.1.6 Analysis:

Settings->Analysis - these settings are used by the Analyze Move/Game/Match or Session command. Note that this is totally different to what is used in the Hint command, which uses the above settings. You probably want at least World Class here. My experience on a 700MHz PC is that a 7 point match takes about 15 to 20 minutes to analyze on the Supremo settings. But the results tend to be very accurate.
